PRIVACY POLICY

1.Information Collection and Use:

• We collect personal information such as name, contact details, and medical history from blood donors and ambulance drivers for the purpose of facilitating blood donations and emergency medical transportation services.

• The information provided by users will only be used for the intended purposes and will not be shared with third parties without explicit consent, except as required by law.

2.Data Security:

• We employ industry-standard security measures to protect the confidentiality and integrity of user data, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.

• Users have the right to access, modify, or delete their personal information stored on our platform, subject to verification of identity.

3.Location Information:

• The app may collect and use location information from users' devices to facilitate blood donor matching and ambulance dispatch services.

• Users have the option to disable location tracking or provide manual location information if they prefer not to share their precise location.

4.Third-Party Services:

• We may integrate third-party services such as mapping and payment processing tools to enhance the functionality of the app.

• Users should review the privacy policies of these third-party services for information on how their data is collected and used.

5.Updates to Privacy Policy:

• We reserve the right to update our privacy policy to reflect changes in legal requirements, industry standards, or app functionality.

• Users will be notified of any material changes to the privacy policy and provided with the opportunity to review and consent to the updated terms
